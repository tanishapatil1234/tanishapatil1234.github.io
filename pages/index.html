Empowering female leaders in San Diego.

Women make up 50% of the American workforce, but only 7.4% of CEOs (Fortune 500).

Welcome to She Leads. Here we aim to bridge the leadership gap and empower female business owners/leaders in the San Diego community.

Spotlight Story: Meet Natalie Gill

California-born and raised, Natalie is Native Poppys leader of all things floral design. She launched her intrepid flower business in 2015, to bring beauty to lifes important moments, and to make others happy. With a deep love of the natural world, Natalie has grown her business into a celebration of all the beauty that nature has to offer.
