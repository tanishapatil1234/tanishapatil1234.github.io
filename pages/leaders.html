Native Poppy by Natalie Gill

California-born and raised, Natalie is Native Poppys leader of all things floral design. She launched her intrepid flower business in 2015, to bring beauty to lifes important moments, and to make others happy. With a deep love of the natural world, Natalie has grown her business into a celebration of all the beauty that nature has to offer. She hopes to encourage her team to step into their creative potential and share their talents with the world.
